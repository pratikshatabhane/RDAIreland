Welcome to RDA Bulk Packaging Ireland Ltd.

RDA Bulk Packaging Ireland Ltd. has all the experience from RDA Bulk Packaging Ltd., who have been successfully supplying a comprehensive range of FIBC’s (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers), also known as Bulk Bags to many different sectors across the UK, for over 23 years and more recently in Europe and other countries worldwide. Our teams are dedicated to supplying you with the best packaging solution for your business. Along with Bulk bags we also supply many other products such as; woven polypropylene sacks, sandbags, garden waste/kerbside collection bags, polythene bags, liners and many more. We hold stocks in our Irish warehouses for both immediate delivery, and also those products that have been manufactured to specific requirements for our existing customers that we have contracted with them to stock on their behalf.

About Us

Your partner in packaging solutions

RDA Bulk Packaging Ireland Ltd. has all the experience from RDA Bulk Packaging Ltd., a privately owned company that has been trading for over 23 years. Our core business is the importation and supply of Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBC’s), otherwise known as bulk bags, tote bags or tay bags. From the very early years in business we have continually re-invested and driven our business to grow the markets and products we supply and now successfully supply products to customers in agricultural, chemical, recycling, waste management, building and many more business sectors.
