Fertilizer Bags

Our NPK Fertilizer Bags, Known As Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) Or Bulk Totes, Are Specifically Designed For The Efficient Storage And Transportation Of Various Types Of Fertilisers, Especially Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), And Potassium (K) Blends. These Bags Are Essential For The Agricultural Industry, Ensuring That Fertilisers Are Safely Handled And Maintained In Optimal Condition.

More About Us

Key Features

• Material: Crafted From High-Grade Woven Polypropylene For Durability, Flexibility, And Tear Resistance. Some Bags Also Include Special Coatings Or Liners For Additional Protection Against Moisture Or Contamination.
• Size And Capacity: Available In Several Sizes, Ranging From Half A Cubic Meter To Two Cubic Meters, With Load Capacities Typically Between 500 Kg And 1500 Kg, Accommodating Large Quantities Of Fertiliser Efficiently.
• Lifting Loops: Equipped With Robust One Lifting Loop Or Two Lifting Loop At The Top For Easy Handling With Forklifts Or Cranes, Ensuring Safety And Convenience During Transportation.
• Fill And Discharge Spouts: Can Be Featured With Fill Spouts For Streamlined Filling And Discharge Spouts At The Bottom For Controlled Emptying, Facilitating Efficient Loading And Unloading Processes.
• Safety Ratings: Designed With A Specific Safety Factor Ratio (E.G., 5:1 Or 6:1), Indicating The Strength And Safe Load Capacity Of The Bags.

Advantages

• Cost-Effectiveness: These Bags Are An Economical Solution Compared To Other Storage Options, Offering Reduced Shipping Costs Due To Their Lightweight Nature.
• Versatility: Our Bags Are Adaptable To Different Industry Requirements And Are Designed To Handle Various Fertilisers.
• Space Efficiency: When Not In Use, These Bags Can Be Folded Flat, Conserving Valuable Storage Space.
• Durability And Strength: Made From Robust Materials, Our Bags Can Transport Heavy Loads Without Compromise
• Ease Of Transportation And Handling: With Lifting Loops, They Are Easy To Move, Enhancing Operational Efficiency.
• Easy To Fill, Discharge, And Handle, These Bags Streamline Operations And Reduce Labour Time. Reduced Risk Of Contamination: The Design Minimises Contamination Risks, Which Is Essential For Maintaining Fertiliser Quality. These Advantages Make Bulk Bags A Highly Effective Solution For A Broad Range Of Material Handling And Storage Needs, Offering Both Practical And Economic Benefits.
