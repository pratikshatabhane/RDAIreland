ABOUT US

Your partner in
packaging solutions

RDA Bulk Packaging Ireland Ltd. has all the experience from RDA Bulk Packaging Ltd.,
a privately owned company that has been trading for over 23 years.
Our core business is the importation and supply of Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBC's),
otherwise known as bulk bags, tote bags or tay bags.
From the very early years in business we have continually re-invested and driven our business
to grow the markets and products we supply and now successfully supply products
to customers in agricultural, chemical, recycling, waste management, building and many more business sectors.
