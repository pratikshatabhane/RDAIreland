RDA Bulk Packaging is pleased to introduce our Food Grade Baffle Bag, a specialised and efficient solution designed to enhance the storage and transportation of bulk food products. These innovative bags incorporate baffle panels that maintain shape, ensuring better stability and improved space utilisation during handling, loading, and unloading operations.

know more

Some -Key Features

  • Baffle Panel Technology: The Food Grade Baffle Bag has internal baffle panels made from durable food-grade materials. These panels create square or rectangular compartments within the bag, preventing bulging and maintaining a consistent shape, even when partially filled. This design optimises space usage, allows secure stacking, and minimises product shifting during transport.
  • Food-Safe MaterialsOur Food Grade Baffle Bags are manufactured using high-quality, food-grade polypropylene resin. The material is carefully selected to ensure it meets food safety standards, protecting your valuable products from contamination and maintaining their quality and freshness.
  • Spacious and Sturdy: These bags are available in various capacities, catering to different food products diverse storage and transportation needs. The robust construction reinforced stitching, and baffle technology combine to provide stability, ensuring your food items are well-protected throughout the journey.
  • Customisable Options:At RDA Bulk Packaging, we understand that each food product has unique requirements. Hence, our Food Grade Baffle Bags are customisable in terms of dimensions, lift loop options, and printing of logos or handling instructions, ensuring a tailored solution for your specific needs.
  • Safe and Efficient Handling: The Food Grade Baffle Bag has sturdy corner lift loops that ensure safe and easy handling during loading and unloading procedures. The balanced weight distribution reduces the risk of accidents, safeguarding your products and preventing damage..
